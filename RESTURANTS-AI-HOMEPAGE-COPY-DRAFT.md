## AI Explained Simply

### Headline

AI should make restaurant marketing easier, not harder.

### Copy

AI can help draft menu descriptions, organize social post ideas, suggest review replies, and turn restaurant details into better website copy.

But the owner stays in control.

The goal is not to sound robotic.

The goal is to sound clear, local, and trustworthy.

- payment setup
- online ordering integration
- live chatbot
- automated SMS
- automated email outreach
- ad spend management
- private customer account system
