# Responsible Play Basics

Slot-style games are entertainment.

They should not be treated as income, a way to solve money problems, or a guaranteed path to winning.

SlotsFreeUSA helps adults understand free slots, social casino basics, sweepstakes-style terms, and casino-style offers in plain English.

We do not operate gambling. We do not accept bets. We do not process payments. We do not promise winnings.

## Start With Free Play

The safest first step is to play free and learn how the game works.

Free play can help you understand:

- game speed
- symbols
- paylines
- bonus rounds
- coin or credit systems
- whether you actually enjoy the game

Do not rush into outside offers before reading the rules.

## Read The Terms Before You Click

Before joining any outside casino-style, social-casino, or sweepstakes-style site, check:

1. age rules
2. location rules
3. bonus terms
4. coin or credit rules
5. playthrough rules
6. expiration dates
7. redemption or withdrawal rules if any
8. support options
9. responsible-play tools

If the terms are hard to find, slow down.

## Set Limits

Before you play, decide:

- how much time you will spend
- whether you will use free play only
- when you will stop
- what signs mean it is time for a break

A limit works best when you set it before you start.

## Take Breaks

Slot-style games can move quickly.

Take breaks often. Step away if you feel rushed, frustrated, or tempted to keep going after you planned to stop.

## Do Not Chase Losses

Do not keep playing because you are trying to win back money, credits, coins, or time.

That is a warning sign to stop.

## Warning Signs

Consider stopping or asking for help if you:

- play longer than planned
- spend more than planned
- hide your play from family or friends
- feel stressed after playing
- try to win back losses
- borrow money to play
- skip bills or responsibilities
- feel unable to stop

## If Play Stops Feeling Fun

Stop immediately.

Take a break. Talk to someone you trust. Use support resources if needed.
