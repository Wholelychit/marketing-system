# Marketing Calendar

This calendar keeps Wholelychit publishing simple, steady, and reusable across all active websites.

## Goal

Publish helpful content every week without starting from zero.

Use one weekly focus, one main content piece, and simple repurposing.

## Weekly Rule

Each week, choose:

1. One project
2. One audience problem
3. One main page or article
4. One short video script
5. Three social posts
6. One Canva asset
7. One review/improvement task

## Weekly Schedule

| Day | Task | Output |
|---|---|---|
| Monday | Pick the focus | Project, topic, keyword, CTA |
| Tuesday | Draft main content | Website page, article, FAQ, or guide |
| Wednesday | Create support content | Video script, social posts, Canva idea |
| Thursday | Publish or prepare | Final page/post assets ready |
| Friday | Review and improve | Links, title, CTA, mobile check |
| Saturday | Light promotion | Repost, pin, community-safe comment |
| Sunday | Plan next week | Keep, improve, or change focus |

## Project Rotation

Use this simple rotation unless one project is urgent.

| Week | Project | Main Goal |
|---|---|---|
| Week 1 | ReadEasy30 | Trust, practice habit, parent/helper clarity |
| Week 2 | PetNeeds.ai | Pet questions, checklists, safe beginner advice |
| Week 3 | SlotsFreeUSA | Beginner guides, honest risk language, entertainment |
| Week 4 | Resturants.ai | Local restaurant value, owner benefits, demo messaging |
| Week 5 | MathEasy30 | Simple math confidence and daily practice |

Then repeat.

## Monthly Minimum Output

Each month, aim for:

- 4 helpful website pages or articles
- 4 short video scripts
- 12 social posts
- 4 Canva graphics or pins
- 1 review pass per active website

## Publishing Checklist

Before marking content ready:

- Title is clear
- First answer appears fast
- Content is easy to read on mobile
- CTA is soft and useful
- Internal links are included
- No unsafe claims
- No income promises
- No medical, legal, financial, or gambling advice presented as certainty
