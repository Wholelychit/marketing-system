A simple math helper tip:

When a learner misses a problem, ask:

"Show me how you got that answer."

That one sentence helps you find the mistake without turning it into shame.

## Comment replies

### If someone asks if MathEasy30 is free

Right now the goal is to make practice easy to start and simple to use.

### If someone asks what grade level it is for

It is not an official grade-placement tool. It is meant for basic math practice, review, and confidence building.

### If someone says their child panics over math

That is common. I would slow the session down, use fewer problems, praise effort, and stop before frustration takes over.

### If someone asks if it replaces tutoring

No. It is a practice resource. A teacher, tutor, or specialist can still be important when a learner needs deeper help.
