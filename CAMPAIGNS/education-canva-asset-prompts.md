## Brand Feel

ReadEasy30 should feel:

- calm
- friendly
- simple
- no-shame
- readable
- beginner-safe
- helpful for adults, parents, tutors, ESL learners, and children

MathEasy30 should feel:

- calm
- practical
- confidence-building
- simple daily practice

## Suggested colors

Use soft, friendly education colors:

- teal
- sky blue
- cream
- white
- soft yellow
- gentle navy text

Avoid harsh red, neon colors, clutter, and tiny text.

## Canva Prompt Style

Use this format when creating a Canva design:

Create a calm education social media graphic for ReadEasy30. Use soft teal, sky blue, cream, and white. Make the text large and easy to read. Include a friendly bubble or book icon. Keep the design uncluttered. The main text is: “[INSERT TEXT]”. Add small footer text: “ReadEasy30.com”.

## Do Not Use

- tiny text
- cluttered stock photos
- scary school imagery
- red error colors
- guaranteed-result claims
- medical or diagnosis language
- pictures that shame learners
