## Day 5 — Reading confidence tip

Reading tip: ask one question at a time.

After a short story, ask:

1. Who was in the story?
2. What happened?
3. Where did it happen?

Simple questions build confidence before harder comprehension work.

Practice here: `https://readeasy30.com/app.html`

## Day 6 — Math confidence tip

Math tip: slow beats rushed.

For a struggling learner, speed can create panic. Let them solve one problem slowly, explain what they did, and then try one more.

Confidence grows from repeatable wins.

MathEasy30: `https://matheasy30.com/`

## Day 11 — Math helper post

If a learner misses a math problem, do not start with "wrong."

Try:

"Show me how you got that answer."

That one sentence turns math into a conversation instead of a fight.
