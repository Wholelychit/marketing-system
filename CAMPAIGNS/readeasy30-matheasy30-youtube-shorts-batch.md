# ReadEasy30 + MathEasy30 YouTube Shorts Batch

Last updated: 2026-05-29

## Purpose

Use these short scripts for YouTube Shorts, TikTok, Instagram Reels, and Facebook Reels.

Keep videos simple: one message, one visual, one action.

## Video style

- 15 to 35 seconds
- calm voice
- large text on screen
- simple background
- no loud panic music
- no shame language
- show one tip at a time

On-screen text:

Reading practice does not need pressure.

Voice:

## Script 5 — One question at a time

On-screen text:

Voice:

If a reader struggles, do not ask five questions at once. Start with one: Who was in the story? Then ask what happened. Small steps build confidence.
