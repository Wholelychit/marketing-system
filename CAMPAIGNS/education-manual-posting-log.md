## Rules

- Post manually until live posting tools and accounts are approved.
- Do not use paid ads yet.
- Do not add tracking pixels yet.
- Do not promise guaranteed results.
- Use calm, no-shame language.
- Record every post so the next week can be improved.

## What to track

For every post, record:

- date
- channel
- post topic
- link used
- asset used
- posted status
- early result
- notes
