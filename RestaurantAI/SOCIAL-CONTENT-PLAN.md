# Restaurant.ai Social Content Plan

## Daily Content Types

### Facebook
- Local restaurant highlights
- Family meal deals
- Community food events
- User food photos

### Instagram
- Food photography
- Reels
- Restaurant interiors
- Desserts
- Short food reviews

### TikTok
- Fast food videos
- Best local meals
- Cheap eats
- AI restaurant picks
- Hidden gems

### Pinterest
- Family dinner ideas
- Date night restaurants
- Best breakfast spots
- Food bucket lists

### YouTube Shorts
- Top 5 restaurants
- Best burgers
- Best pizza
- Best breakfast
- Cheap meals under $10

## Posting Goal

Start with:
- 2 short videos daily
- 2 image posts daily
- 1 local article weekly

## Content Strategy

Focus on:
- local trust
- visual food appeal
- easy recommendations
- family-friendly tone
- mobile users

## Growth Strategy

- Encourage photo uploads
- Encourage reviews
- Use local hashtags
- Feature small businesses
- Build community trust
