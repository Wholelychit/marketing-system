# Wholelychit Marketing Operating System

Professional one-person marketing system for five websites:

- `readeasy30.com`
- `matheasy30.com`
- `petneeds.ai`
- `slotsfreeusa.com`
- `restaurantaibot.com`

## Purpose

This file is the central layout for planning, producing, publishing, automating, and measuring marketing work.

The system is built for one person using AI, free tools, and low-cost tools.

## Operating Principle

Do not market all five sites equally every day.

Use a production rotation:

1. Build one main site this week.
2. Keep one support site warm.
3. Reuse one content idea across article, video, social, and outreach.
4. Measure results weekly.
5. Improve the highest-potential page or channel first.

## Production Model

```text
Research -> Content Brief -> Article -> Short Video -> Social Posts -> Outreach -> Measure -> Improve
```

## Weekly Production Layout

| Day | Main Job | AI Help | Human Time Needed |
|---|---|---|---|
| Monday | Pick site, goal, keyword, and audience problem | Draft strategy and topic list | 15 minutes review |
| Tuesday | Produce article or landing page copy | Draft article, FAQ, meta title, meta description | 20 minutes review |
| Wednesday | Produce short video scripts | Draft 3 scripts and captions | 15 minutes review |
| Thursday | Create Canva/visual assets | Generate image/video prompt plan | 20 minutes review |
| Friday | Publish/schedule | Create social captions and checklist | 15 minutes review |
| Saturday | Outreach/light sharing | Draft safe outreach messages | 20 minutes review |
| Sunday | Scorecard review | Summarize wins, misses, next action | 15 minutes review |

## Site Rotation

| Week | Main Site | Reason | Support Site |
|---|---|---|---|
| Week 1 | ReadEasy30 | Strongest trust-building education product | MathEasy30 |
| Week 2 | PetNeeds.ai | Broad topic and monetization potential | ReadEasy30 |
| Week 3 | RestaurantAIbot | Local business lead potential | PetNeeds.ai |
| Week 4 | SlotsFreeUSA | Entertainment/search traffic potential | RestaurantAIbot |
| Week 5 | MathEasy30 | Education sister-site growth | ReadEasy30 |

Then repeat based on scorecard results.

## Core Metrics

Each site gets one main conversion goal.

| Site | Main Conversion Goal | Secondary Goal | Watch Metric |
|---|---|---|---|
| ReadEasy30 | Start lesson app | Read parent/tutor guide | Returning visitors |
| MathEasy30 | Start math practice | Use speak/help feature | Practice starts |
| PetNeeds.ai | Read helpful pet guide | Click resource/affiliate/local option | Top pages by search clicks |
| SlotsFreeUSA | Read beginner guide or review | Click safe provider/info link | Click-through and engagement |
| RestaurantAIbot | Request demo/contact | Read restaurant growth guide | Local business leads |

## Minimum Weekly Output

For the main site each week:

- 1 article, guide, or landing page improvement
- 3 short video scripts
- 5 social posts
- 1 Canva graphic set plan
- 1 outreach list idea
- 1 scorecard update

For the support site each week:

- 1 small improvement
- 1 social post
- 1 internal link idea

## Production Priority Rule

Work in this order:

1. Fix the site message.
2. Make the user action obvious.
3. Add one useful page.
4. Repurpose it into video and social.
5. Track the result.
6. Improve the winner.

## Starting Recommendation

Start with ReadEasy30 as the first full marketing build.

Reason:

- The UX and instruction files are strongest there.
- The product has a clear mission.
- It can support parents, tutors, adult learners, ESL learners, and struggling readers.
- It can produce many helpful content topics without paid ads.

Second: PetNeeds.ai.

Third: RestaurantAIbot.

Fourth: SlotsFreeUSA.

Fifth: MathEasy30, unless MathEasy30 is needed as a sister-site bridge for ReadEasy30.

## No-Waste Rule

Do not create content that has no destination.

Every content idea must become at least one of these:

- website page
- article
- FAQ
- video script
- social post
- outreach draft
- Canva asset
- scorecard entry
