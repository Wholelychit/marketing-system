# PetNeeds.ai Category Content Plan

Last updated: 2026-05-29

## Purpose

Use this plan to build PetNeeds.ai into a helpful pet-care content site with clear categories, practical answers, safe vet guidance, future affiliate planning, and local sponsor/ad opportunities.

## Core Promise

Clear pet help for everyday owners.

PetNeeds.ai should help people understand what to do, what to watch for, and when to call a veterinarian.

## Safety Rule

PetNeeds.ai must not pretend to replace a veterinarian.

Use this style:

- “This is general pet-care information.”
- “Call your veterinarian if symptoms are serious, sudden, painful, or getting worse.”
- “If your pet may have eaten something toxic, contact a veterinarian or poison-control resource right away.”

Avoid:

- diagnosis claims
- treatment guarantees
- emergency certainty
- medication dosing
- “this is safe” statements without current verification
- telling people to delay vet care

## Category Hub Structure

Each hub should include:

1. short plain-English intro
2. “start here” links
3. common questions
4. safety warning box
5. product/resource disclosure note if monetized later
6. local-service note if local ads are planned later
7. internal links to related hubs

## PetNeeds.ai Page Template

```md
# [Page Title]

## Quick Answer

Give a short safe answer in 2–4 sentences.

## Why This Matters

Explain the pet-owner problem in plain language.

## What To Do First

List safe basic steps.

## What To Watch For

List warning signs.

## When To Call a Veterinarian

Include clear vet guidance.

## Helpful Supplies

List general supplies only. Do not add live affiliate links until approved.

## Common Questions

Add 3–5 FAQs.

## Final Reminder

This page gives general pet-care information. It does not replace a veterinarian.
```

## Standard Vet Warning Box

Use this box on health/safety pages:

> **Pet safety note:** This page gives general pet-care information. It does not replace a veterinarian. Call your vet if symptoms are serious, sudden, painful, or getting worse.

## Emergency Style Warning

Use only when appropriate:

> **Urgent note:** If your pet may have eaten something toxic, is having trouble breathing, is bleeding heavily, cannot stand, has repeated seizures, or seems severely distressed, contact a veterinarian or emergency pet clinic right away.

## Product / Affiliate Disclosure Draft

Use only when product links are added later:

> PetNeeds.ai may earn a commission from some product links. We aim to recommend useful pet supplies and explain when a veterinarian or qualified pet professional should be contacted.

## Local Sponsor Disclosure Draft

Use only when local ad/sponsor placements are added later:

> Some local listings or sponsor placements may be paid placements. Pet owners should still check licenses, reviews, services, availability, and fit before choosing a provider.
